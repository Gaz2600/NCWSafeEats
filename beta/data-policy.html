Data, Accuracy & Disclaimer

Data sources

NCW Safe Eats intends to use restaurant inspection data provided by local public health agencies (for example, the Chelan–Douglas Health District) via public records or open data exports.

The site does not perform inspections or assign scores. It only displays information from official sources in a more user-friendly way.

Limitations & timing

  • There may be a delay between an inspection and when it appears on this site.
  • Data can occasionally be incomplete, corrected, or updated by the health district.
  • Historical records may be missing or summarized depending on what is provided.

For the most current or authoritative information, please refer directly to the official public health department.

Not an official agency site

NCW Safe Eats is an independent community project and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially sponsored by any city, county, state, or health department.

Nothing on this site should be interpreted as medical advice or an official determination of safety. It is a helpful tool, not a replacement for official information.

Reporting corrections

If you believe a restaurant’s information is incorrect or incomplete, you can:

  • Submit a “Data Issue” on the project’s GitHub issue tracker, or
  • Email: contact@ncwsafeeats.com

When possible, please include details such as the restaurant name, city, and links or references to official records.

Privacy

NCW Safe Eats does not require accounts or collect personal information from visitors. Basic, anonymized traffic data may be logged by GitHub Pages, Cloudflare, or other hosting infrastructure for security and performance purposes.

No user-specific tracking, profiling, or advertising cookies are intentionally used by this site.

Use at your own risk

By using this site, you acknowledge that:

  • Information may not always be complete, current, or error-free.
  • You are responsible for your own decisions about where to dine.
  • The maintainers are not liable for actions taken based on information presented here.
