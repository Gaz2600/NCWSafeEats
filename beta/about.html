About

What is NCW Safe Eats?

NCW Safe Eats is a community-built, independent site that makes food safety inspection information easier to read for North Central Washington – including Wenatchee, East Wenatchee, Chelan, Leavenworth, and surrounding areas.

Why this project exists

Restaurant inspection data is public, but it's often hard to find and even harder to interpret. NCW Safe Eats aims to:

  • Present inspection results in a clear, searchable format
  • Help people – especially those who are medically vulnerable – make informed choices
  • Encourage transparency and trust between the public, restaurants, and health authorities

Where the data comes from

Inspection data is intended to come from the Chelan–Douglas Health District (or other applicable public health agencies) using publicly available records or datasets they provide. NCW Safe Eats does not generate or alter official inspection scores – it only reformats and displays the information.

Until we receive and integrate official data, this site is running in a limited “beta” mode and may contain placeholder or sample entries that are not representative of real inspections.

Independent, not official

NCW Safe Eats is not affiliated with any county, city, or health department. It is a community project that relies on official public records but is maintained separately.
